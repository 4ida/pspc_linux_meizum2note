# https://wiki.postmarketos.org/wiki/Porting_to_a_new_device#Introduction
Depending on your selection, pmbootstrap may ask you to analyze a boot.img file to get offset values required for the flash method. 
Get the right one for your device from TWRP (twrp-*.img), LineageOS (boot.img inside the zip archive) or by extracting it from within a running Android. 
If you can't find any, you can skip this step for now by just pressing the return key.
